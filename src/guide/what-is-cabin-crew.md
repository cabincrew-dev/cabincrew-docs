# What is Cabin Crew?

Cabin Crew is the **Universal Chain of Custody** for Autonomous Workflows.

We provide the infrastructure to certify the identity of AI Agents, authorize their flight plans through policy enforcement, and cryptographically seal the evidence.

## Why do you need it?
*   **Compliance:** Prove to auditors what your AI did.
*   **Security:** Prevent supply chain attacks via unverified engines.
*   **Control:** Enforce OPA policies on every decision.

## The Stack
1.  **The Orchestrator:** BSL 1.1 Binary.
2.  **The Protocol:** Apache 2.0 Standard.
3.  **The Engines:** Open Source workers.
